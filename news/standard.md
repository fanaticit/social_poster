Complete the Upgrade Rush Quest & Take On a Riftcharged Monster!

Hunters,

Get ready for another Upgrade Rush Quest, where you can earn Zenny and monster materials!
During this event, you can collect items such as Shining Eggs, which can be exchanged for Zenny, through rewards from monster hunts, Hunt-a-thons, and Elder Dragon Interceptions.

In addition, you’ll be able to collect even more monster materials thanks to increased large monster slay rewards and special limited-time quest rewards!

During the same period, a riftcharged monster will appear in Base Defense.
Gather the materials you’re missing, upgrade your equipment, and take on the riftcharged monster!

Dates & Times
Monday, January 12th, 2026, at 9:00 a.m. – Sunday, January 18th, 2026, at 11:59 p.m. (local time)

Additional Information
All hunters who have completed the prologue can enjoy the event content.
Event quests will automatically become available in the Special Quests tab to all hunters who have completed the prologue.
Riftborne monsters will only start to appear after you have unlocked 8★ monsters on the field.
Bazelgeuse will appear more frequently in volatile territories.
During the event, Deviljho will not invade in volatile territories.
Volatile territories will not occur more frequently during the event.
Appearances of volatile territories on the field can be unlocked by completing the pre-season story.
The pre-season story refers to the period from the end of the prologue to the completion of Chapter 13: Sunderer of the Skies.
Event quests will be available from Monday, January 12th, 2026, at 9:00 a.m. – Sunday, January 18th, 2026, at 11:59 p.m. (local time). Be sure to complete the quests and receive your rewards between these times.
Quests and rewards are subject to change without notice.
