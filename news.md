Check Out the December Event Lineup!

Hunters,

Read on to find out what’s coming up in December!

Season 8 kicks off this month! In this update, more monsters, including Astalos, will make their debut in Monster Hunter Now. A brand-new feature called Base Defense is coming to exploration bases, and with it, riftcharged monsters!
Check out the full details of Season 8 here.

Before Season 8 begins, we’ll be hosting the Season 7 Climax event, where you can continue hunting the monsters introduced in Season 7 and collect Mysterious Driftstones. Make the most of the final days of the season!

And as we head into the holidays, get ready for A Happy Hunting New Year 2026. New event-exclusive equipment will make its appearance, bringing seasonal style to your winter hunts.

Let’s close out the year with some epic hunts and celebration!

December Event Lineup
Season 7 Climax Event
Dates & Times

Part I: From Monday, December 1st, 2025, at 9:00 a.m. until Friday, December 5th, 2025, at 8:59 a.m. (local time)
Part II: From Friday, December 5th, 2025, at 9:00 a.m. (local time) until Wednesday, December 10th, 2025, at 11:59 p.m. (UTC)

Look forward to more appearances of Espinas, who made its debut in Season 7, as well as Brute Tigrex, Lunagaron, and Garangolm! The featured monsters will differ between the first and second parts of the event.
You’ll also have the chance to obtain Mysterious Driftstones J, K, and L, which first appeared in Season 7.

This event marks the final week of the ongoing 4-Week Challenge (11/10–12/9).
From Dec. 5–9, a special bonus ticket will be available for purchase, offering even more ways to enjoy the event! Stay tuned for more details.

Season 8 Kickoff Event
Dates & Times

From Thursday, December 11th, 2025, at 12:00 a.m. until Sunday, December 21st, 2025, at 2:59 p.m. (UTC)

The appearance rates of monsters coming in Season 8—Astalos, Goss Harag, Quematrice, and the newly arriving Riftborne Khezu—will be increased! Additionally, equipment forged from Astalos, Goss Harag, and Quematrice materials will receive performance boosts during the event.

Weapons forged from Astalos and Khezu materials are designated as “featured defense weapons”, granting bonus points when equipped during riftcharged monster hunts. Be sure to forge and upgrade them to prepare for riftcharged monsters!

For details on when riftcharged monsters will appear, check the Exploration Base & Monster Outbreak Schedule.

A Happy Hunting New Year 2026
Dates & Times

Part I: From Monday, December 22nd, 2025, at 9:00 a.m. until Monday, December 29th, 2025, at 8:59 a.m. (local time)
Part II: From Monday, December 29th, 2025, at 9:00 a.m. until Sunday, January 4th, 2026, at 11:59 p.m. (local time)

This large-scale event will take place over the year-end and New Year period, divided into two parts. Look forward to exclusive event equipment and layered equipment, as well as a brand-new event quest, where all hunters work together toward a shared goal.
Stay tuned for more details!
